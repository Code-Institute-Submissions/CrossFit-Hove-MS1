What is CrossFit ?

The Workouts

CrossFit workout of the day (or WOD) are a mixture of aerobic exercises, calisthenics and Olympic weightlifting. An hour-long class will include a warm-up, a skill development segment (typically Olympic liftiing) and finish with the high-intesity WOD. Addtional classes are offered. Every week day has an open gym hour and Saturdays provide speciality classes.

Functional Movement

The movements that we use are functional (replicated in life, requiring no special machinery or equipment). We dont look to train individual muscles in isolation but instead use compound (multiple joint) movements which mirror our movements outside of our training. Training is progressive and difficulty is increased as you grow in strength.
