What is CrossFit ?

The Workouts

CrossFit workout of the day (or WOD) are a mixture of aerobic exercise, calisthenics and Olympic weightlifting. An hour-long class will include a warm-up, a skill development segment (typically Olympic lifts) and the high-intesity WOD. Addtional classes are offered, every week day has an open gym hour and Saturdays provide speciality class.

Functional Movement

The movements that we use are functional (replicated in life, requiring no special machinery or equipment). We dont look to train individual muscles in isolation but instead use compound (multiple joint) movements which mirror our movements outside of our training. Training is progressive and difficulty is increased as you grow in strength.

Community

Your support network. Our classes are small and intimate where our CrossFit Certified Coaches not only know your name, but also your capabilities. We are one of the original 250 global affiliates out of now more than 15,000 !!! Crossfitters encourage and motivate each other in every class as they work toward their goals.
